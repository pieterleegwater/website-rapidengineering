AUTOMATISERING

The goal of RapiD Engineering is to relieve engineers of repetitive work. An engineer should be busy with creative solutions and product improvements. Every minute they spend on repetitive work is one too many. We therefore help companies to relieve their engineers by building software tools for CAD software. For example, we make product configurators with which anyone can generate a complete drawing package of a product of their choice in no time at all. We also automate complete engineering processes where communication between other software (such as ERP) and CAD software can also be realized. These automation tools can be built for all CAD applications that have an API.
